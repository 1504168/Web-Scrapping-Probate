Conditions of Use and Privacy Policy

\n

 

\n

Usage of this site constitutes acceptance of the terms and conditions in this disclaimer.

\n

The Remote Case Access System (“RCAS”) is maintained by the Clermont County Probate Court. The RCAS is intended as a convenience to the citizens of Clermont County for the purpose of obtaining public record information regarding cases filed in the Clermont County Court of Common Pleas, Probate Division. No warranty or guarantee is provided or implied through the availability of the RCAS, and no certification of authenticity can be assumed regarding the accuracy of the information provided by the RCAS.

\n

Every effort is being made to ensure the accuracy of the information in the RCAS. The docket entries found in this system are the same as those on the official court docket in the Clermont County Court of Common Pleas, Probate Division.

\n

The information contained in this system does not constitute the official record and, being a copy, may have some inaccuracies. If questions ariseregarding the accuracy of information in the RCAS; the official record in the original court file at the courthouse should be consulted. Data and information from the RCAS is not intended for distribution by other persons, entities or organizations and the Office of the Clermont County Probate Court accepts no responsibility for any use of the information beyond the purposes stated in this disclaimer. The Court, their agents, and developers of this web site assume no liability whatsoever associated with the use or misuse of the data contained herein.

\n

Users creating their own hyperlinks to pages on this site are requested and cautioned to do so in a manner which maintains a clear distinction between the user\'s content and official content published by this system. Users packaging pages from this web site hyperlinked within frames are requested and cautioned to properly identify RCAS provided content. Users formulating or constructing their own search or query URLs in attempting to bypass the database query parsers and directly access the database search engines or documents will be permanently denied access to the database without notice, as such efforts may lead to incorrectly formulated queries which may jeopardize proper operation of the database servers.

\n

Under no circumstances, including negligence, shall anyone involved in the creating and maintaining of this system be liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages or lost profits that result from the use or inability to use the Clermont County Court of Common Pleas, Probate Division web site and/or any other web site linked to this site. In the event this system is unavailable for any reason, including but not limited to technical difficulties, the Clermont County Court of Common Pleas, Probate Division assumes no responsibility to restore the RCAS within any predetermined period of time.

\n

Due to limitations of equipment and bandwidth, The RCAS is not intended as a source of information for those seeking copies of large quantities of records. These databases are intended for use by the general public. They are notintended to be a source for bulk downloads of data. Efforts to mine large quantities of data from the RCAS without prior approval of the Clermont County Court of Common Pleas, Probate Division will be detected and stopped.
